## Decision Trees
* [cite_start]**Structure**: Interpretable logic models driven by entropy and information gain[cite: 7].
* [cite_start]**Entropy ($H$)**: A measure of uncertainty or randomness in a set of data[cite: 7].
* [cite_start]**Information Gain (IG)**: Measures the reduction in entropy after a dataset is split on an attribute[cite: 7].
* [cite_start]**Inductive Bias**: A preference for shorter trees over longer ones[cite: 7].
* [cite_start]**Overfitting**: The primary danger in ML where a model fits the noise in training data but fails to generalize to new data[cite: 7].
