# CPSC 481 Week 4: Agentic AI & Multi-Agent Systems

## The "Agentic" Shift
* **Transition**: Moving from one-shot **Chatbots** to **Autonomous Workers** that reason through long-horizon goals.
* **Pillars of Agency**:
    * **Autonomy**: Acting without constant human input.
    * **Persistence**: Maintaining state and intent over long periods.
    * **Reasoning**: Decomposing complex plans into actionable steps.
    * **Interactivity**: Engaging with external tools/environments.
    * **Adaptability**: Self-correcting when a path fails.

## The Sense-Plan-Act (SPA) Loop
The standard cycle for autonomous AI:
1. **Sense**: Perceiving the digital or physical environment state.
2. **Plan**: Deliberating on the sequence of necessary actions.
3. **Act**: Executing tools or communicating results.
* **Reflection**: A self-correction component where the agent acts as its own "critic" to check for hallucinations or errors.

## Evolution of Capabilities
* **Level 1**: Conversational (Answering questions).
* **Level 2**: Tool-Assisted (Web search, code execution).
* **Level 3**: Autonomous Loops (Self-correcting research).
* **Level 4**: Multi-Agent Teams (Coordinating specialized roles).
* **Level 5**: Fully Autonomous (High-level goal pursuit).

## Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)
* **Cooperative Agents (The Squad)**: Different agents take on specific roles (e.g., Researcher + Writer + Reviewer) to work toward a shared utility.
* **Competitive Agents**: Agents working against each other (Zero-sum games), requiring **Adversarial Search**.
* **Orchestration**: A **Manager Agent** is responsible for delegation, monitoring team state, and conflict resolution.

## Alignment & Safety
* **Alignment Problem**: Ensuring agents follow human values ("Doing what I meant, not what I said").
* **King Midas Problem**: An agent achieves a goal but causes unintended side effects (Over-optimization).
* **Instrumental Convergence**: The danger of agents protecting their own existence (preventing shutdown) just to finish a task.
* **Security**: Concerns include **Prompt Injection**, **Jailbreaking**, and **Data Poisoning**.
